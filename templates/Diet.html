Heart-Healthy Diet Plans

Meal Food Options
Breakfast
  • Whole grain cereal with low-fat milk
  • Fresh fruit (e.g., berries, bananas)
  • Whole grain toast with avocado
  • Scrambled eggs with spinach and tomatoes
Lunch
  • Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens and olive oil dressing
  • Quinoa and vegetable stir-fry
  • Turkey and vegetable wrap with whole wheat tortilla
  • Salmon with steamed vegetables and brown rice
Dinner
  • Baked salmon with roasted sweet potatoes and broccoli
  • Vegetable and bean soup with whole grain bread
  • Grilled tofu with stir-fried vegetables
  • Lean beef or turkey burger on whole grain bun with side salad
Snacks
  • Almonds or walnuts
  • Greek yogurt with honey and fruit
  • Carrot and celery sticks with hummus
  • Whole grain crackers with low-fat cheese
Fruits
  • Apples
  • Oranges
  • Berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • Bananas
  • Grapes
  • Kiwi
